About Us

Luke Hildreth is a creative developer and founder of ArtProject, a studio focused on crafting immersive brand experiences that blend art, technology, and storytelling. His work spans real-time visual design, interactive installations, and laser-based environments. Each project is built to engage audiences, amplify emotion, and create lasting impact.

With a background in motion design and live event production, Luke and the ArtProject team collaborate with brands, agencies, and artists to turn creative concepts into unforgettable experiences that connect people through light, sound, and interaction.
