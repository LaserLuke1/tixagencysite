In collaboration with Ubisoft and Lucasfilm Games, ArtProject brought Star Wars Outlaws to life with a large-scale projection mapping installation for the game's release event in Irvine, California.

The exterior of the venue was transformed into a cinematic canvas, merging live visuals, laser light, and dynamic motion design inspired by the Star Wars universe. Every frame was tailored to capture the grit, energy, and atmosphere of Outlaws — from its neon-lit cityscapes to the expansive desert worlds that define its visual identity.

ArtProject handled the full projection mapping pipeline — from content design and show programming to on-site calibration and playback. The result was a seamless fusion of brand storytelling and technical precision, delivering an unforgettable first look at one of the most anticipated titles of the year.
