Client

Services

Interactive Displays
Event Production

Year

Location

The Challenge

Subaru needed to stand out in the crowded auto show environment while showcasing their latest vehicles and brand values. The activation needed to create an engaging experience that would attract visitors, educate them about Subaru's technology and safety features, and drive meaningful engagement in a competitive exhibition space.

Our Approach

We created an immersive brand experience featuring interactive technology demonstrations, virtual reality experiences, and hands-on vehicle exploration zones. The activation highlighted Subaru's commitment to safety, adventure, and environmental responsibility through engaging multimedia presentations and experiential touchpoints that allowed visitors to connect emotionally with the brand.
